Ceramic Coatings

Ceramic coatings create a durable, chemically resistant layer over the vehicle’s clear coat, helping to preserve the finish beneath it. This protective barrier improves gloss and hydrophobic behaviour while making routine washing safer and easier.

How Ceramic Coatings Are Booked

Ceramic coatings cannot be booked as standalone services. They must be added to a paint correction package to ensure the paint is properly prepared before application.

Start by booking either the Gloss Enhancement Correction or the Deep Correction Detail, then select your ceramic protection option below.
