Paint Correction

Paint correction is the process of machine polishing the paintwork to improve gloss, clarity and depth while reducing visible defects such as swirl marks, light scratches, oxidation and dullness. Each paint correction package includes full exterior decontamination and interior cleaning before polishing begins.

The goal is safe improvement, not unnecessary removal of clear coat.

01

Gloss Enhancement Correction

Price R1,800 – R2,200
Duration 1+ days
Interior Included

The Gloss Enhancement Correction is a single-stage machine polish designed to improve the overall appearance of the paintwork. It is ideal for vehicles with light swirl marks, minor dullness or paint that needs more gloss and clarity.

Includes

  • Full exterior decontamination package
  • Interior package
  • Single-stage machine polish

Best For

  • Light swirl marks
  • Dull paint
  • Gloss improvement
  • Newer or well-maintained vehicles
  • Visible improvement without heavy correction

This is not a full defect-removal service. Results depend on paint condition and safe correction limits.

02

Deep Correction Detail

Price R2,800 – R3,500
Duration 2+ days
Interior Included

The Deep Correction Detail is a two-stage machine polishing process designed for vehicles with heavier swirl marks, oxidation, deeper defects and reduced paint clarity. The first stage focuses on defect reduction, while the second stage refines the finish for improved gloss and depth.

Includes

  • Full exterior decontamination package
  • Interior package
  • Two-stage machine polish
  • Stage 1 — cutting
  • Stage 2 — refining

Best For

  • Heavier swirl marks
  • Oxidised or dull paint
  • Deeper visible defects
  • Vehicles needing serious correction
  • Pre-ceramic coating preparation

Defect removal depends on paint condition, paint depth, defect depth and safe correction limits.
