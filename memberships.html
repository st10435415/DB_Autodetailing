DB AutoDetailing

Monthly Memberships

DB AutoDetailing memberships are designed for clients who want their vehicle maintained consistently throughout the month. Instead of booking once-off services every time, members receive a set number of monthly maintenance cleans based on their chosen package.

How It Works

Simple by design.

01

Choose your category

Ceramic or non-ceramic — based on whether your vehicle already has ceramic protection. Ceramic-coated vehicles require different maintenance products and are easier to maintain.

02

Select your tier

Standard, Higher, or Top — based on how frequently you need your vehicle maintained throughout the month. All tiers include a monthly decontamination service.

03

Billed monthly

Schedule your services within the month at your convenience. All membership packages include interior cleans as part of each service.
