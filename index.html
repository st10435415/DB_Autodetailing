Our Standard

We don't wash cars. We protect them — with the patience, products, and process the work actually deserves.

From the daily-driven to the rarely-driven, every vehicle that arrives at DB AutoDetailing receives the same uncompromising standard of care. Two-stage paint correction. Gyeon ceramic coatings. Honest pricing. Lasting results.

About the Founder

Built on a love for the craft.

DB AutoDetailing started where most things worth doing start — with one car, one weekend, and the stubborn belief that there had to be a better way. Years of obsessing over products, technique, and the small details that separate a wash from a true detail turned a hobby into a calling.

Today, every booking is handled personally. No production line. No shortcuts. Just careful, patient work using the right products, and the kind of attention you'd expect from someone who treats every vehicle as if it were their own.

If you're here, it's because the standard you're looking for is higher than a quick wash. That's exactly what DB AutoDetailing was built to deliver.

— Derek Blake Founder, DB AutoDetailing
