DB AutoDetailing

Exterior Detailing

Our exterior detailing services are designed to safely clean, refresh and decontaminate your vehicle's exterior. Whether you need a regular maintenance wash or a deeper decontamination service before polishing or protection, each process is carried out with care to reduce the risk of wash-induced marks and improve the overall appearance of the vehicle.

01

Essential Exterior Wash

Price R250 – R350
Duration Up to 2 hours

The Essential Exterior Wash is a safe maintenance wash designed for vehicles that need a proper exterior clean without full decontamination. This service removes loose dirt, road film and surface grime while leaving the vehicle clean, dry and refreshed.

Includes

  • Wheel and rim clean
  • Alkaline pre-wash
  • pH-neutral hand wash
  • Safe drying process
  • Exterior windows
  • Tyre finish

Best For

  • Regular maintenance cleaning
  • Lightly dirty vehicles
  • Clients wanting a refreshed exterior
  • Vehicles not needing decontamination

02

Full Exterior Decontamination Detail

Price R600 – R800
Duration Up to 4 hours
Interior Included

The Full Exterior Decontamination Detail is a deeper cleaning service designed to remove bonded contamination from the vehicle's paintwork. This includes tar, iron fallout and embedded surface contamination that normal washing cannot remove. The service also includes an interior clean, making it a more complete detailing package.

Includes

  • Wheel and rim clean
  • Alkaline pre-wash
  • pH-neutral hand wash
  • Tar removal
  • Iron fallout removal
  • Clay bar treatment
  • pH-neutral rinse
  • Safe drying process
  • Exterior windows
  • Tyre finish
  • Interior package included

Best For

  • Vehicles with rough paintwork
  • Exposure to road grime, tar or brake dust
  • Pre-polishing preparation
  • Pre-ceramic coating preparation
  • A deeper clean inside and outside

This package does not include machine polishing or paint correction.
