Interior Detailing

Our interior detailing service is designed to refresh the cabin and improve the cleanliness of the surfaces you interact with every day. The focus is on carpets, plastics, leather, fabric areas and interior glass.

01

Complete Interior Detail

Price R300 – R500
Duration Up to 2 hours

The Complete Interior Detail is a focused cabin cleaning service for clients who want the inside of their vehicle cleaned properly. It targets dust, dirt, light staining and general interior build-up across the main cabin surfaces.

Includes

  • Full vacuum
  • Carpet clean with drill brush and chemicals
  • Leather clean and condition
  • Fabric / Alcantara clean where applicable
  • Plastic clean
  • Interior windows

Best For

  • Regular interior maintenance
  • Dusty or lightly soiled interiors
  • Leather and plastic refresh
  • Vehicles needing a cleaner, fresher cabin

Excessive pet hair, sand, mud, stains, mould, smoke smell or heavily neglected interiors may require an additional quote.
